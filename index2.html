Virtual Drum Kit allows us to play a drum anytime, anywhere by a press of a key!

This, of course, falls short compared to the real deal.

However, this project allows us to get an idea of how

drums work. You would still be able to learn a

thing or two, and maybe come up with your own harmonic symphony!
